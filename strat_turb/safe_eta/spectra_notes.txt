The horizontal spectra files are written to columns in the following order, 
1 : k_x
2 : k_y
3 : E(u_x)
4 : E(u_y)
5 : E(u_z)
6 : E(u)
7 : E(B_x)
8 : E(B_y)
9 : E(B_z)
10: E(B)
11: E(Temp)
12: E(Chem)

The vertical spectra files are written to columns in the following order, 
1 : k_z
2 : E(u_x)
3 : E(u_y)
4 : E(u_z)
5 : E(u)
6 : E(B_x)
7 : E(B_y)
8 : E(B_z)
9 : E(B)
10: E(Temp)
11: E(Chem)
